Crystal Collector Game

How to Play!

In the grey box is your target number.

There are four color rupees below. By clicking on a rupee, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Win the game by making your total score equal to the random number generated in the grey box.

The value of each rupee is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each rupees.
